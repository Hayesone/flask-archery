Have-a-Go Sessions

We can offer a chance to have a go at Archery using our equipment and instruction from qualified coaches. Coaches are DBS checked and have a minimum of level 1 coaching qualification through Archery GB.

The sessions last for an hour, all equipment is provided for you. The hour starts with a safety briefing, and you will start shooting within 15 minutes. During the hour our coach will be on hand to guide and help you.

Have a go session are for anyone aged 10+. We can accommodate up to 4 people per coach at any one time. You can shoot Recurve, Compound or Long Bow.
