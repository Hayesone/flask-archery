Arrow Building

All our arrows are custom made to your specific requirements by a competent archer with significant experience of making arrows. We sell all types of arrows from basic entry level to high performance Olympic standard.

Arrows are not sold “off the shelf”. Due to the number of different models, spine ratings and colours available it is not possible for us to stock everything so, if you are thinking of getting new arrows, we need to know what fits you. If you don’t already know what you need, please come in and talk to us. Be sure bring your bow with you when you come so we can correctly measure your draw length and draw weight as the rating on the limbs is rarely the actual draw weight being held at full draw.

During the appointment we will take the measurements and calculate the correct spined arrows for you. We will discuss fletching type and colour and other options that are available to you like customised wraps on your arrow shafts to make them more unique.
